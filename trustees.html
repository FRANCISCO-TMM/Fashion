Governance

Board of Trustees

The board meets quarterly to oversee the house's creative direction, finances and archive, sustainability commitments, craft standards, and relationships with long-standing clients. Select a name to read their full note.

ÉF

Madame Élise Fontenoy

Chair · Creative Direction

Has overseen the house's silhouette and seasonal direction since 2003.

Madame Fontenoy joined as a junior pattern-cutter in 1994 and has chaired the board since 2009. She still sits in on first fittings for every bridal commission and maintains final say on any silhouette leaving the cutting room.

HC

Mr. Henri Castellane

Trustee · Finance & Heritage

Oversees the house's accounts and the long-term upkeep of the pattern archive.

Before joining the board, Mr. Castellane spent eleven years auditing independent ateliers across France. He treats the pattern archive as a financial asset in its own right and has resisted three separate proposals to digitise it without a paper backup.

AT

Dr. Aiko Tanaka-Reyes

Trustee · Sustainability

Leads the house's sustainable silk sourcing programme, now in its third year.

Dr. Tanaka-Reyes holds a doctorate in textile science and previously consulted for two French silk mills on water reclamation. She joined the board specifically to build the silk programme and reports its progress to the full board every quarter.

JB

Mr. Joaquín Belmonte

Trustee · Atelier & Craft

Trained in Lunéville embroidery and sets the house's hand-finishing standards.

Mr. Belmonte trained under a Lunéville embroidery master for six years before joining the house. He now trains every new embroiderer in the workshop personally and has final approval over any new hand-finishing technique adopted by the ateliers.

PV

Ms. Priya Varma

Trustee · Client Relations

Oversees the appointment calendar and long-term client correspondence.

Ms. Varma manages the relationships behind the house's longest-standing commissions, some spanning three generations of one family. She set the current appointment structure, including the rule that a first sitting is never rushed past forty-five minutes.

LH

Mr. Lukas Hartmann

Trustee · Archive & Heritage

Maintains the physical pattern ledger and the house's historical records.

A trained archivist, Mr. Hartmann catalogued the house's first thirty years of patterns into the current ledger system. He can locate any client's original pattern, by hand, in under ten minutes — a claim the rest of the board has tested more than once.
